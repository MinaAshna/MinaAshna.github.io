Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02/02/2025

Timeloom ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

Data Collection and Usage

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Email addresses
  • User IDs
  • Location data
  • Contact information

No Marketing or Data Tracking

The App does not engage in any marketing, advertising, or user-tracking activities. There are no third-party services that collect or analyze user data.

Data Storage and Deletion

All data generated by the App is stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers. When you delete the App, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

No Data Linked to the User

The App does not link any data to individual users. There is no account creation, login, or user identification required to use the App.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
